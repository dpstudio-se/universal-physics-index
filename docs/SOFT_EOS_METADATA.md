# Soft-EOS-Compatible Metadata

Soft-EOS is a response/topic-routing policy, not a tokenizer or model-weight modification. Recurring low-criticality topics may receive compact delta updates; novelty, uncertainty, drift, evidence conflict, bubble risk or increased criticality trigger fuller review.

Future records may expose novelty, uncertainty, drift, evidence-conflict and criticality scores plus `last_reviewed`. v0.1.0-alpha does not implement AI routing.
