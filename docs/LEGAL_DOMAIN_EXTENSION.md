# Future Legal Domain Extension

Legal material is outside UPI's scientific core and must never be encoded as a physical constant. A future `domain = law` record should include jurisdiction, primary legal source, chapter, section, version date, court/authority, interpretation status, primary-source URL, uncertainty and whether the statement is settled law or argument.

Swedish concepts such as Tryckfrihetsförordningen, Yttrandefrihetsgrundlagen, Regeringsformen and offentlighetsprincipen require current primary-source verification. This document makes no legal conclusion.
