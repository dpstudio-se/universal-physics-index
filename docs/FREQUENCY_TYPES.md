# Frequency Type Safety

Every stored frequency must declare one of the `FrequencyType` values: photon, angular, oscillation, sampling, repetition, resonance, carrier, biological rhythm, symbolic reference, numerical normalization or rest-mass frequency.

The number 8 Hz may describe a measured oscillation, biological signal, electromagnetic frequency or symbolic coordinate. Equal numbers and units do not make these meanings interchangeable. Angular frequency additionally obeys `ω = 2πf`; it must not be silently substituted for ordinary frequency.
